About Company

Transforming Clinical Practice

Molecular Diagnostics Nigeria Limited is a unique b2b firm which plays a pivotal role in the entire value chain of molecular testing in Nigeria and beyond

At MDNL, we commit ourselves to the provision of advanced molecular testing services, suited to the medical practice needs of the twenty-first century, improving patient health through a complete range of in vitro diagnostics solutions for the management of diseases and targeted cancers

Mission

To provide the best affordable, reliable, and advanced molecular testing services and equipment.

Vision

To become Africa's leading consulting firm in pathology and molecular testing.

Scope

We commit ourselves to the provision of advanced molecular testing services,suited to the medical practice needs of the twenty first century.

Quality Products and Services

Whether we are engaged to design and deliver a new lab, upgrade an existing facility, supply diagnostic products or engaged to provide subject matter expertise and advice on strategies or policies, we always ensure that whatever solutions we provide do not only have quality and sustainability at their core but are always focused on our clients' end-users.
